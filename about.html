about

This app gives you a feedback of your event, This app is made using VanillaJS to get know whether a event organized by you is successfull or not. This app simply take the count of likes and dislikes given by user and gives the feedback on the event.

This feedback is divided into three cases-

case 1 :

If likes are greater than dislikes, it will show output Event is Successful

case 2 :

If dislikes are greater than likes, it will change output Not refreshing

case 3 :

In case likes and dislikes are equal, this app suggest that Waiting for more feedback
